Afrobeats: The Global Takeover

We dive deep into how West African rhythm and soul became the world's most dominant sound in the 21st century.

Listen Now

Show Notes & Summary

In this episode, Sarah Nkosi sits down with industry experts to trace the evolution of Afrobeats. We look back at Fela Kuti's foundation and how it transformed into the chart-topping hits of Wizkid, Burna Boy, and Tems.

Key Discussion Points:

  1. The Digital Transformation: From local radio to global streaming.
  2. The UK Connection: How the diaspora fueled the fire.
  3. Identity & Language: Singing in Pidgin and Yoruba for a global audience.
